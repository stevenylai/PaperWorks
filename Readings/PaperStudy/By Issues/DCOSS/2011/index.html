IEEE DCOSS 2011 Sponsors

Technical Committee on Parallel Processing

IEEE DCOSS '11

June 27 - 29, 2011

(Main Conference - June 27 and 28 / Workshops - June 29)

Barcelona, Spain

Final program (PDF)

The 7th IEEE International Conference on Distributed Computing in Sensor Systems (IEEE DCOSS '11) will take place at the Casa Convalescència in Barcelona, Spain during Monday, June 27 - Wednesday, June 29, 2011. .

DCOSS 2005 (Marina del Rey, California), DCOSS 2006 (San Francisco, California), DCOSS 2007 (Santa Fe, New Mexico), DCOSS 2008 (Santorini Island, Greece), DCOSS 2009 (Los Angeles, California, USA) and DCOSS 2010 (Santa Barbara, California) featured high quality research papers and interesting invited and contributed posters. IEEE DCOSS '11 is intended to cover several aspects of distributed computing in sensor systems such as high level abstractions and models, systematic design methodologies, signal and information processing, algorithms, analysis and applications.

The conference will be co-located with several closely related workshops, and will provide a forum for researchers and practitioners to present their contributions related to the above high-level aspects of distributed sensor systems. In addition to contributed papers, the meeting will also include keynote addresses by leading researchers, a panel discussion, and a poster session.
